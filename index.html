Privacy Policy (App & Website)

🔒 Important Privacy Notice

All your data stays on your device! Noise Protocol Professional does not automatically transmit any personal data to our servers. Your privacy is our highest priority.

Data Processing Overview

This app was developed to create and store noise protocols exclusively locally on your device. We use the principles of data minimization and privacy-by-design.

What Data is Processed?

Locally Stored Data

  • Noise Protocols: Date, time, location, decibel values, descriptions
  • Media: Audio/video/photo recordings (only when actively used by you)
  • GPS Coordinates: Only when you activate location services
  • App Settings: Language, noise types, preferences

No Data Transmission

The app does not transmit any of your personal data to external servers, except when:

  • You consciously export data (e.g., via email)
  • You use Google Play Billing for premium features
  • Crash reports (anonymized, only with your consent)

Permissions and Their Purpose

Microphone Permission: Decibel measurement and audio recordings for noise protocols. Processing exclusively local on your device.

Camera Permission: Photo and video recordings as evidence. Please observe personality rights.

Location Permission: GPS coordinates for noise protocol localization. Only during app usage.

  • Complaint to supervisory authorities
